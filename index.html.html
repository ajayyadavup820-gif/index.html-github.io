About us

A Tribute to a Visionary Leader

The Fraternity of FS University pays tribute to our esteemed patron, Late (Shri) Fulan Singh Ji, whose values and ideals continue to inspire us. His unwavering belief in the power of education has shaped the foundation of our institution, and his legacy lives on in every student who walks through the gates of FS University. In 2004, Dr. Dilip Yadav set out to fulfill the vision of his beloved father, Late (Shri) Fulan Singh, by establishing institutions dedicated to providing quality education to society and beyond. FS University stands as a tribute to his legacy, initiated under the Fulan Singh Jan Kalyan Trust, with the mission of making Shikohabad, Uttar Pradesh, a recognized hub for knowledge seekers from all walks of life. The FS University fraternity proudly honors and pays tribute to our esteemed visionary patron, Late (Shri) Fulan Singh Ji.

Courses

A.N.M (Auxiliary Nurse Midwife)

ANM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwife) is a diploma-level healthcare course focused on basic nursing, maternal care, and community health services. The program mainly trains students to provide primary medical care, assist in childbirth, care for mothers and newborns, and support vaccination and health awareness programs. ANM professionals play an important role in rural and urban healthcare centers, government hospitals, clinics, and community health programs. After completing the ANM course, candidates can work as auxiliary nurse midwives, healthcare assistants, or community health workers, and they may also continue higher studies in nursing for better career growth.

The course offers various specializations such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Electronics, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. During the B.Tech program, students gain both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience through laboratories, projects, internships, and industrial training.
